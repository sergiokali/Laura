"The Weight of Us" — Lighting Cues

Concept: A contemporary duet exploring the development of a human relationship and the profound privilege of reliance. The visual atmosphere uses stark, cooler lighting (blues/steels) to represent separation and individuality, transitioning into enveloping, warmer lighting (ambers/golds) to depict togetherness, shared weight, and reliance.

Fade Out Spots
Shifting Awareness
At the slight change in music, the white spotlights fade away, leaving only the general cold blue wash.
Symbolizes: The breaking of their isolated bubbles. As the stark highlights disappear, they are placed in the same shared, albeit cold, environment, signaling the beginning of mutual awareness.

The Fracture
The light changes back to cold everywhere at the last note of the section.
Symbolizes: A sudden moment of separation, misunderstanding, or fear. The abrupt return to a cold atmosphere represents the loss of that initial connection and the harsh reality of being alone again.

Rebuilding Trust
The lighting slowly transitions into warm lighting as the dancers come together and get closer physically and emotionally.
